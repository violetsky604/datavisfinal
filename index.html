About This Dataset

This dashboard explores 40,881 trending video entries from YouTube Canada, spanning November 2017 to June 2018 across 16 categories. The dataset captures what Canadians were watching, liking, and engaging with at scale.

Each entry represents a video that appeared on the Canadian trending page, including view counts, engagement rates, publish dates, and category tags. The goal is to surface patterns in what drives visibility — and whether reach actually equals reaction.
